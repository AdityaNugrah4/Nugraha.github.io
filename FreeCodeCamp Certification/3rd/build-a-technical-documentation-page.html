Introduction

Damage, often abbreviated as DMG, is dealt when a character or enemy attacks its target.

There are two types of DMG in Zenless Zone Zero, Standard DMG and Attribute Anomaly DMG, which have different methods of calculation.

Zenless Zone Zero Damage Calculation

General Damage Formula

Standard Damage

Standard DMG is dealt directly by agent skills.

DMGStandard = Base DMGAttacker × DMG Bonus MultiplierAttacker × CRIT MultiplierAttacker × DEF MultiplierTarget × RES MultiplierTarget × DMG Taken MultiplierTarget × Stunned MultiplierTarget

Attribute Anomaly Damage

Attribute Anomaly DMG is dealt when triggering Burn, Shock, Corruption, Shatter, Assault, or Disorder.

DMGAttribute Anomaly = Anomaly Base DMGAttacker × Anomaly Proficiency MultiplierAttacker × Anomaly Level MultiplierAttacker × DMG Bonus MultiplierAttacker × DEF MultiplierTarget × RES MultiplierTarget × DMG Taken MultiplierTarget × Stunned MultiplierTarget

Multiplier Detail

Base Damage

The stat that determines Base DMG is always ATK unless otherwise stated.

Base Damage = Ability Multiplier × Stat

Damage Bonus Multiplier

DMG Bonus may be specific to an Attribute, a type of skill, a type of attack type (e.g. Slash, Strike, Pierce), or other conditions. Different sources of DMG Bonus are calculated additively.

DMG Bonus Multiplier = 1 + Total DMG Bonus

CRIT Multiplier

CRIT Multiplier = 1 + CRIT DMG if attack scores a CRIT hit

or

CRIT Multiplier = 1 if otherwise

and

CRIT MultiplierAverage = 1 + CRIT Rate × CRIT DMG

DEF Multiplier

Enemy DEF at level 1 is usually between 36 and 60. They have the same growth pattern as the Level Factor.

DEF Multiplier = Level FactorAttacker / max((DEFTarget × (1 - PEN RatioAttacker) - PENAttacker),0) + Level Factor Attacker

RES Multiplier

RES Multiplier = 1 - RESTarget + RES ReductionTarget + RES IgnoreAttacker

Damage Taken Multiplier

DMG Taken is not to be confused with DMG Bonus. Despite similar in-game wordings, DMG Bonus effects are applied to the attacker, while DMG Taken effects are applied to the target.

RES Multiplier = 1 - RESTarget + RES ReductionTarget + RES IgnoreAttacker

Stunned Multiplier

The Stunned Multiplier varies by enemy, and the value can be seen under the enemy's Daze bar when they are Stunned.

Anomaly Base Damage

Anomaly Base DMG = Anomaly Multiplier × ATK
Attribute Anomaly Anomaly Multiplier
Burn 50%
Shock 125%
Corruption 62.5%
Shatter 500%
Assault 713%

Disorder occurs when a new Attribute Anomaly replaces an existing one. The Anomaly Multiplier of Disorder depends on the type of Anomaly that has been replaced as well as the amount of time that has passed (in seconds) since its application.

Attribute Anomaly Anomaly Multiplier
Burn 450% + floor((10 - Time Passed) × 2) × 50%
Shock 450% + floor(10 - Time Passed) × 125%
Corruption 450% + floor((10 - Time Passed) × 2) × 62.5%
Shatter 450% + floor(10 - Time Passed) × 7.5%
Assault 450% + floor(10 - Time Passed) × 7.5%

Anomaly Proficency Multiplier

Anomaly Proficency Multiplier = Anomaly Proficency × 0.01

Anomaly Level Multiplier

Anomaly Level Multiplier is truncated to 4 decimal places.

Anomaly Level Multiplier = 1 + 1/59 × (level - 1)
